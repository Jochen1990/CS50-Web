About Polly

Polly is a simple survey web app which allows a user to create a survey with up to 4 possible answers (and a minimum of two answers).

Once the survey is created, it appears under 'all surveys'. Also, it is added to your profile, so you can see which surveys you have created in total.

The link 'all surveys' takes you to a route which displays all surveys created by you or other users. A user is able to vote on each survey only once. The number of total votes/voters, is displayed under each survey.

Once the author of the survey decides to close the survey (and only he or she is capable to close the survey), the survey can be reached via the route 'closed surveys - results'.

There, you can see all closed surveys along their number of total votes and the respective results.
